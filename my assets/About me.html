About me

Hello, I'm Mr. Nolan A. Notarte, a versatile professional with a robust background in technical roles
spanning various industries. Armed with a Bachelor of Science in Industrial Education majoring
in Electronics Technology and having successfully passed the Licensure Examination for Teachers
in 2013, I have continuously pursued higher education to expand my knowledge base.

My professional journey began as an Electronics Technician, Biomedical Technician, and Instrumentation
Technician across multiple companies, providing me with a solid technical foundation and problem-solving
skills.

Transitioning into the realm of education in 2014 at Lourdes School of Mandaluyong was a significant
milestone. As a high school educator in Electronics and Computer Technology, I cultivated an environment
that nurtured curiosity and innovation among students.

Recognized for my dedication, I was appointed Subject Area Coordinator for Technology and Livelihood
Education in 2019. In this role, I revitalized educational programs, driving technological advancements
and ensuring academic excellence within the institution.

Stepping further into educational leadership, I assumed the role of Instructional Technology (IT) Coordinator
in 2021. Here, I spearheaded initiatives to integrate technology into the school's curriculum, fostering a
dynamic and progressive learning environment.

Additionally, I completed a Master's degree in Learning Management at Saint Paul University Manila.
This advanced education equipped me with comprehensive insights into learning methodologies and management
strategies, enhancing my ability to innovate and lead in the educational landscape.

Driven by an unwavering passion for growth and exploration, I am currently delving into web development.
I am enthusiastic about merging my diverse skill set, combining technical expertise, educational leadership,
and newfound web development skills, to pioneer innovative solutions and enhance educational experiences.

I am excited about the prospect of transitioning into a career in web development while leveraging my varied
experiences and educational background to make meaningful contributions in this dynamic field.
